The Happiness Chronicles: Exploring the Key Drivers of Global Well-Being

Welcome to the Happiness Chronicles

This interactive narrative visualization allows you to explore the World Happiness Report data in three scenes:

  • Scene One: A global overview of happiness scores.
  • Scene Two: Trends in happiness scores from 2016 to 2019.
  • Scene Three: An in-depth look at factors influencing happiness scores for selected countries and years.

Use the tabs above to navigate between scenes and explore the data. Each scene provides unique insights into global well-being, trends over time, and the key drivers of happiness.
